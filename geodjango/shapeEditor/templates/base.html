Welcome to our simple WebGIS platform for Marine Habitat Mapping. With this platform, you can import, edit, process and export your Esri shapefiles. At the moment, you can only create sediment distribution maps or so called sedimentary habitat maps as is shown in slide show below. In order to create this kind of maps, you will need to upload a shapefile containing gran size percentage values of gravel, sand and mud contents of your sediment samples. You can also upload other shapefiles to examine and make simple edits in this platform.
